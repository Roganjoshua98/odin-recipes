Yellow Mac

Description

It's the tastiest pasta dish in the world! Just like my mother makes it, and probably no one else's!

Ingredients

  • However much pasta you desire
  • 2x eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil some water in a kettle. While waiting, put your desired amount of pasta in a pot and add a few pinches of salt.
  2. Pour boiling water into pan and leave on for the time specified on the pasta packaging
  3. Crack two eggs into a bowl and beat them
  4. Grate some parmesan and mix in with the eggs
  5. When pasta is boiled, drain the pot and then put the pasta and egg mix in
  6. Place on an unheated surface and stir the pot until the sauce has your preferred consistency (time will depend on the heat, and if you think it's taking too long you can apply some low heat for a very short time)
